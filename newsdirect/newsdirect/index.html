What is News Direct?

Introducing  an entirely new, purpose-built news and content distribution platform that provides powerful performance advantages, for strategic communications professionals.

We identified the most pressing needs of a market which has undergone seismic change in recent yearsby commissioning an extensive, independent, market assessment in July 2019.

Those insights were combined with the deep knowledge and experience of founder, Gregg Castano - former president of industry leader Business Wire and 32-year industry veteran.

The end result gave us the blueprint we needed to design and build a platform that maximizes today's technology to provide the new features and streamlined workflow that newswire users told us they require in this evolving media environment.

What makes News Direct different?

News Direct has transformed the entire newswire user experience with advanced automation, cloud technology, custom software and A.I. This dynamic, flexible platform significantly improves the system current newswire users have grown to accept for lack of a better alternative.

We've systematically reimagined age-old newswire practices regarding workflow, security, pricing, customer support and analytics while introducing visionary new features that have created a higher standard for news and content distribution and greater performance advantages for users.

We still do all the things traditional newswires do - but have raised the bar with streamlined efficiency, ease of use, speed and automation. We do so much more that it will change your view of the capabilities and limitations of newswires as you've known them.
